Pregnancy is an amazing time in a woman's life. It was also a tense moment when a mysterious new life to grow and develop in the womb. Once pregnancy occurs, various effects occur in the female body; both effects due to hormonal changes, body shape, as well as the emotional state of women become pregnant.

Along with the growth of the fetus in the womb, emerging signs that indicate pregnancy. In pregnancy, changes occur in the entire female body, especially on tools external and internal genitalia, as well as on the breast (mammary). This is because the role of somatomamotropine, estrogen, and progesterone hormone in pregnancy.

In addition to physical changes, pregnant women will also experience psychological changes, which are also influenced by hormonal changes. These changes interact with internal and factors affecting transition period of a pregnant woman to be a mother. Often, we hear a woman saying how happy she was due to be a mother. However, not infrequently there are also women who are concerned about pregnancy, worried about losing her beauty, or the possibility of the baby is abnormal.

As a professional, the midwife should perform their role and function properly. It is very important to examine changes in the physical and psychological that happened, so that midwives can provide care, support, and help addresses the concerns of pregnant women. Therefore, the midwife should encourage pregnant women to antenatal check routine (antenatal care). The main purpose of antenatal care is to facilitate healthy and positive outcomes for both mother and baby in a way to build a trusting relationship with the mother, early detection of complications that can be life-threatening, preparing for the birth, and providing education.

  2. Psychological improvement in pregnancy

    3. During pregnancy, most of women experience psychological and emotional changes. Often we hear a woman say how happy since becoming a mother, and that he had already picked out a name for the baby to be born. However, not infrequently there is a woman who feared that a problem occurred in her pregnancy, worried that she might lose her beauty, and the possibility of the baby is abnormal.

    1. First trimester (1-3 months)

      2. Soon after conception, levels of progesterone and estrogen in pregnancy will increase. This will cause nausea and vomiting in the morning, weakness, fatigue, and breast enlargement. She felt unwell and often hates her pregnancy. Many mothers feel disappointment, rejection, anxiety, and sadness. Often at the beginning of her pregnancy she hoped not to get pregnant.

      In the first trimester, mother will always look for the tent-pins to be assured that she is indeed pregnant. Any changes that occur in the body will always be considered carefully, because his stomach is still small, pregnancy is the secret of a mother who may be told to others / to be her sleeve.

      The desire to have sexual intercourse on a woman in the first trimester is different. Although some women experience higher sexual arousal, most of them decreased libido during this period. This situation creates a need to communicate openly and honestly with your partner. Many women feel the need to be loved and feel strong to love, but without sex. Libido is influenced by fatigue, nausea, breast enlargement, concerns, and fears. All of this is a normal part of pregnancy in the first trimester.

      The first reaction of a man, knowing that he would become the father is the emergence of pride in his ability to have children mixed with concern over the readiness to become a father and make a living for his family. A prospective father may be very concerned about the state of women who are pregnant and start avoiding sex for fear of harming the baby. There is also a man who is sexually against pregnant women is relatively larger. In addition to the response he showed, a father should be able to understand the situation and accept it.

    2. Trimester II (4-6 months)

      3. In the second trimester, usually mother feels healthy. The mother's body is already accustomed to higher hormone levels and discomfort due to pregnancy is reduced. Mother's stomach is not too big so it has not perceived as a burden. Mother was receiving her pregnancy and began to use energy more constructively and mind. In this trimester is also the mother can feel the movement of the baby and the mother began to feel the presence of the baby as an outside of himself. Many mothers feel apart from a sense of anxiety, discomfort as she felt the first trimester and feel increased libido.

      She felt more stable, self-regulate better ability, conditions or circumstances more pleasant mothers, mothers are getting used to the physical changes in her body, the fetus is not too large so as not cause inconvenience. Mother had begun to accept and understand about her pregnancy.

    3. The third trimester (7-9 months)

      4. The third trimester is often called the period of waiting and watchful because at that moment the mother felt impatient waiting for the baby born. Baby's movement and abdominal swelling are two things that will remind her about baby. Sometimes the mother was concerned that her baby will be born at any time. This situation causes mother increase alertness and the occurrence of the onset of signs and symptoms of baby delivery. Mother often feel worried or afraid when the baby to be born abnormal. Most mothers will protect her baby and avoid people or anything that it deems harmful to the baby. A mother may begin to feel fear of pain and physical hazards that would arise at the time of delivery.

      Discomfort from pregnancy happen again in the third trimester, and many mother find themselves strange and ugly. Moreover, the mother began to feel sad because it will be separated from her baby and loss received special attention during pregnancy. In this trimester mothers need information and support from her husband, family, and midwives.

      The third trimester is a time of active preparation for childbirth and parenthood. Families began to guess the sex of the baby (whether male or female) and would like anybody. In fact, they may also choose a name for her baby. Maternal weight increases, the pressure on the internal organs, their feeling uncomfortable because the fetus is getting large, the change in self-image (self, no steady, feel disconnected, unloved, uncertain, scared, happy because the baby's birth.
